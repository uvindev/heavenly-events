## 1. BRAND VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES

### Core Brand Voice

Heavenly Events speaks with the **authority of 18 years** and the **warmth of a trusted partner**. The tone is:

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Confident** | They are Sri Lanka's best. Never apologetic, always assured. |
| **Warm** | Like a senior colleague, not a corporate machine. |
| **Precise** | "18 years" not "many years". "500+ events" not "lots of events". |
| **Inspirational** | Every event is a masterpiece. Every moment matters. |
| **Sri Lankan Pride** | This is Sri Lanka's premier brand — written with that pride. |

### Writing Rules

```
✅ Use: "We craft" / "We orchestrate" / "We deliver"
✅ Use: Specific numbers — 18 years, 500+ events, 10,000+ guests
✅ Use: Action-oriented CTAs — "Register Free" / "Explore Events" / "Plan Your Event"
✅ Use: Sensory language — "unforgettable", "captivating", "seamless"

❌ Avoid: "We try to..." / "We hope to..."
❌ Avoid: Generic filler — "world-class solutions" without specifics
❌ Avoid: AI-sounding phrases — "Delve into", "Embark on a journey"
❌ Avoid: Passive voice — "events are organized by us"
```
